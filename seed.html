SEED TO SHELF:

At Global Green Company we believe in creating sustainable communities by building long-lasting relationships starting right at the level of the farmer. We support 20,000 farmers and their families in India and Hungary, year on year by providing a consistently profitable source of income. The seed to shelf model is the bedrock of making Global Green Company a socio-economically successful business.

We guide farmers right from the sourcing of seeds all the way through building sustainable farming models that are profitable and yield produce that makes its way to store-shelves and consumers tables. We ensure resource-control for, and have an on-ground dedicated support team working with farmers all-round the year, making us a leader at farm-sourcing globally.

By enabling our farmer communities with wide ranging disruptive technologies that are easy to implement such as low cost drip irrigation and fertigation techniques, handpicked trellised crop to ensure uniform sizing, R&D operations to monitor seed quality, best-in-class cultivation practices, new crop development and GMO free product standards, we help to empower them and transform their livelihoods.

For the rapid processing of raw materials, and in order to preserve their nutritional value, the growers and farmers we work with are located in areas close to our production and processing facilities, ensuring that our products are always packed fresh.

An unstinting emphasis on continuous research and education has ensured that we always strive to adhere to the highest quality standards.
