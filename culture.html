OUR CULTURE:

Global Green Company is a great place to work at. At all our locations, we have strived to create a fun-filled, natural and productive work ambience filled with like-minded individuals where you do not feel stressed when working, amidst an abundance of greenery.

We hire people who are dynamic and determined, and we favor ability over experience. We strive to maintain an open culture, in which everyone is a hands-on contributor and feels comfortable sharing ideas and opinions.
