Tify Story:

Tify is the in-house brand of Global Green Company. Launched in 1996 and distributed only in India, Tify brings to its consumers a range of premium gourmet foods that help in making meal times happier – both as cooking aids and as accompaniments. Tify sells in both the B2B and B2C segments.

In the B2B segment, Tify caters largely to the HoReCa and QSR channels with products that are essential ingredients in their recipes. These include chunky salsa, pizza and pasta sauces, mayonnaise, relish, tomato paste/puree, pizza toppings, sandwich fillings, sandwich spreads, olives, and processed fruits and vegetables such as gherkins, jalapeños, red paprika, cherry and litchi.

The products are available in food service packs – pouches (multi-layered high oxygen barrier pouches), PET jars and cans.

In the consumer segment Tify provides a variety of products for consumption on the table. The Tify range consists of accompaniments such as olives, salsa, sauces, and spreads, ketchup, pizza pasta sauce and mayonnaise based bread spreads.
